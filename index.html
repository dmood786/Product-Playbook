Product Lifecycle Practice

The discipline that ensures products are intentionally discovered, efficiently built, and continuously improved to deliver measurable value across the health system.
Mandate
From idea to measurable value, systematically
The Product Lifecycle Practice ensures that all data, analytics, and AI products are grounded in real user needs, aligned to enterprise priorities, and delivered through a disciplined lifecycle. It establishes repeatable methods for discovery, prioritization, delivery, and iteration—so that product investments translate into sustained operational impact.
Success metrics
  • % of initiatives with validated discovery before build
  • Time from idea → production (cycle time)
  • Adoption and active usage of delivered products
  • Value realization vs. business case
  • % of reusable product components
